Number Guessing Instructions:

  1. Guess the number that appears on the dice after a roll.
  2. Enter your guessed value into the box and click "Roll".
  3. If guessed correctly then 1 point is added to your score.
  4. Press the end button to end the game and show your total score.

Even/Odd Guessing Instructions:

  1. Guess whether the number that appears on the dice is even or odd after a roll.
  2. Enter either 1(Odd) or 0(Even) and click "Odd/Even".
  3. If guessed correctly the 1 point is added to your score.
  4. Press the end button to end the game and show your total score.
